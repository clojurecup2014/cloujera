Cloujera.

Cloujera lets you do a fine-grained search for spoken words in Coursera's videos

Try searching for "quicksort", "post modernism" or even "globular cluster"

Cloujera was built in 48 hours using Clojure, ClojureScript, Enlive, Om, ElasticSearch and a lot of sweats, tears, trial and error.
